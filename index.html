About CART

Empowering Minds, Building Futures

The Center for Academic Resource and Training (CART) is a strategic institutional unit of Modibbo Adama University, Yola, responsible for academic and professional capacity development through certificate programs, diploma programs, workshops, and specialized training services.

CART is committed to excellence in training, research support, and academic resource development for national growth. Our programs are designed to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring graduates are job-ready and globally competitive.

Our Structure

Units & Departments

CART operates through a well-structured set of units dedicated to delivering excellence in administration, academics, and support services.

Academic Unit

Overseeing all academic programs, curriculum development, and quality assurance across CART.

Accounts

Responsible for financial management, budget planning, and payment processing within CART.

Secretariat

Handles official correspondence, documentation, records management, and administrative coordination.

Administration

Oversees the day-to-day operational management of CART's facilities and human resources.

ICT Support

Provides technology infrastructure, systems maintenance, and digital learning support services.

Admissions & Registration

Manages student admissions, enrollment processes, program registrations, and student records.

Research & Development

Drives innovation through research initiatives, publications, and academic collaborations.

Partnerships & Outreach

Builds strategic partnerships with industry, government, and international institutions.
